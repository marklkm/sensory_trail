# The Sensory Trail at Trinity College Botanic Garden

### Overview

The Sensory Trail is an initiative designed to enhance well-being through sensory engagement with nature. Visitors can explore 18 designated stops, each focusing on different sensory experiences such as sight, smell, and sound. Developed by a student, the trail emphasizes seasonal features, calming elements, and interactive participation.

### Features

- 18 sensory stops covering various aspects of the garden
- Designed for mindfulness and connection with nature
- Year-round exploration

### Accessibility

Although some areas may pose challenges for those with mobility limitations, ongoing efforts are being made to improve accessibility.
