Physics Lawn

The Physics Lawn is the idea of John Parnell Jnr. People can scan a QR code outside the rear gates to bring up the map and allow them to browse the trees, beds, pond etc. If you hover the tree number / tree marker it will display species information, size, carbon stored and a picture.
